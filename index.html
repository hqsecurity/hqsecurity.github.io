Standard Operating Procedure

New Officer Training

Introduction

The duty of a security officer is to secure the property of Electrolux. To accomplish this, an officer

  • observes and reports notable events
  • controls access to Electrolux properties
  • conducts regular patrols of those properties

and assumes other key duties, as directed.

This manual outlines the basic skills every new officer needs upon completion of their 3-day training period. Therefore, senior officers should use this manual as a checklist for training new officers, so that no essential skills are overlooked.
